Welcome to Formatron.

Not much to see here right now as the documentation is very much a work in progress. However the aim of Formatron is to take the pain out of deploying AWS CloudFormation stacks and configuring EC2 instances with Chef. Reducing the volume of configuration by applying sensible and secure defaults where appropriate.
